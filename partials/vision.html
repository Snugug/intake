Vision Statement

From Project Vision

Vision statement provide a single grounding point for all decisions needed to create a successful product. Vision statements help guide a project by answering the following questions:

  • Who will use, buy, or consume the product?
  • Who is the target customer?
  • Who will administer and maintain the product?
  • What needs will the product address?
  • What attributes are critical to success?
  • How does the product compare with existing products?
  • What are the unique selling points?
